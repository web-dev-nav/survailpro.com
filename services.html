Security Services Compliance Auditing

We will assign a senior member of our team to attend your event and audit the security team you have hired. We audit and report to you that your security requested needs are met (the number of guards you requested are performing the duties you requested throughout your event), and that they are compliant with security regulations.

Important: This is only available if SurVail is not performing guard services at your event and performing this service on your behalf would eliminate SurVail from performing guard services at your event in the future. Our intention is not to develop guard services contracts at your event. Our intention is to make sure you are getting the security services that you are paying for.
