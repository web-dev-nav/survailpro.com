Meet Don Vail

Beginning in 1972, Don has attained over 42 years of experience in the Justice, Protection and Security Training sectors. His dedication to excellence and professional integrity has made SurVail a trusted name in Southern Ontario security services.

Experience & Achievements

Discover Don's remarkable journey through decades of security excellence and professional achievement.

1982-1991

Wayne Gretzky Sports Classic

Security Co-Director

Don became one of two security co-directors on the executive committee of the Wayne Gretzky Sports Classic. Responsibilities included the development and management of all aspects of security for the annual event that was attended by up to 40,000 patrons.

Over 100 celebrities from television, movies and sports took part in competitive events including tennis and baseball.

2003-2016

Mohawk College

Faculty/Program Coordinator

Don was employed by Mohawk College as a faculty/program coordinator in the Protection Security and Investigation Program within the Justice Studies Sector.

He hired over 300 students of the Mohawk Justice Sector to work alongside him at numerous security related events. Don considered these Mohawk students amongst the best trained guards in the Ontario Security Industry.

June 2005

Emergency Management

Emergency Management Training

Don successfully completed the Basic Emergency Management Trainers Course - Emergency Management Ontario.

After the course, Don instructed Basic Emergency Management at Mohawk College for several years, sharing his expertise with the next generation of security professionals.

Dec 2012

San Diego, CA

Special Events Management

Don attended the Centre for Police Organizational Studies in San Diego California, successfully completing the "Special Events Management Course" for Law Enforcement and Special Event Officials.

Don then developed and instructed the Special Events Security Planning Course at Mohawk College, bringing cutting-edge knowledge to students.

Since 2013

Event Security

Large Scale Events

Don has worked with various security companies supplying security to large scale concerts, festivals, and sporting events in Ontario. Don has been involved in the management and supervision of over 500 concerts and events at the largest entertainment venues in Ontario, establishing himself as a leader in event security management.

2016-2020

Company Formation

SurVail Protection Services

2016 SurVail Protection Services was launched

2020 Later incorporated to become SurVail Protection and Investigation Services Inc.
