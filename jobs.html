How Our Employment Process Works

Simple, flexible, and designed with your schedule in mind

Email Notification System

As a project is contracted to SurVail, we will let our enrolled candidates know by email. You can accept, or turn it down at that time. A confirmation will be sent in both cases. The next time there is an opening we send you a new invitation.

Above Average Pay

We pay licensed guards above going rates, based on experience, and time of employment with SurVail. If you are interested fill out our online form. You can request removal as candidate at any time. Before applying you can contact Don for any questions you may have.
